Welcome to the Project-We

Project-We is the platform to connect with people all over the world, who share the same passion as you do. Here you can upload your Dreams, Ideas and Visions to find the right team members for your Projects. Project-We is based on the belief, that the shared passion in a team is the key to success. Project-We offers the space to connect, to share, to inspire and to be inspired. To grow and to learn. It's a space to manifest your dreams into reality.

Every idea is welcome. Be it to strengthen the relation between Japan and Switzerland or the curiosity and interest to work with prisoners. Maybe you want to make your home-town to the most colorful city in the world, or you like to strengthen your project working skills and share your knowledge with passionate people. It doesn`t matter if you have already a concept or if you`re in the phase of inspiration and dreaming. Upload what you have, post it, share it and build a solid foundation.
